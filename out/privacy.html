Privacy Policy

    1 Introduction

    This Privacy Policy explains how AVR EDGE NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED (EDGE) collects, uses, and shares information of the personal identity of visitors to our web site www.getEDGE.ai and users of our on-demand recruitment application called HIREalchemy (service). The use of information collected through our services shall be limited to the purpose of providing those to our customers. By using the site and/or services, customers consent to the collection, use and disclosure of any personally identifiable information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. EDGE is a leading skill development and technology solutions provider. Our vision is to continuously and constantly develop skills to empower industries and human resources with products, solutions and services that engage in skill development. EDGE’s ability lies in understanding the recruitment domain and being able to find, score and rank candidates and internal employees to their best fit respective jobs. If case of any questions, concerns or complaints regarding our privacy policy or practices, please contact us at privacy@getedge.ai.

    EDGE identifies itself as a processor and act on behalf of our controller organizations. We do not process any personal information for our own business purposes ourselves that is collected by our controller organizations. Our services include algorithms to process information but the decision to process comes from the controller organizations through contracts when it comes to use of our services and platforms. However, EDGE may process the location information of the website visitors to know from where they are visiting our website and to understand certain other statistics like the time, they spend that will give us an understanding of their interest that may help us as well as the visitor of the website.

    We only provide a talent acquisition platform to our customers and services to customers. EDGE provides its customers real choice and gives them freedom to control the usage of their data.

    EDGE is dealing with two types of personal information:

    1. Directly collected using EDGE website

    2. Indirectly processed using EDGE’s platforms collected by the respective customers (controllers)

    This privacy policy primarily addresses the first type of information and covers the second type of information more from a processor perspective.
