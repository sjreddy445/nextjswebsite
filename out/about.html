Our Story - The Journey To Where We Are Today

Asking The Right Questions

  • The idea of EDGE was sown by asking the right questions and listening to the answers attentively
  • And the most challenging question in that space and time was how to connect a desired candidate to the apt job?
  • What did organizations need to support the change?

In Pursuit of Simplifying Talent Decisions

  • The quest to simplify these talent decisions helping companies and individuals spot opportunities and create possibilities, inspired our CEO, Arjun Pratap, to embark on this exciting but perilous journey
  • The first product prototype Jscore was built
  • Amidst the positive reviews, the drive to make the product better continued relentlessly

Gaining Value & Momentum

  • Soon, our Machine Learning algorithms with self-learning capabilities were introduced
  • During this phase, EDGE received overwhelming support from the National Skill Development Corporation of India (NSDC)
  • We gained recognition at the NASSCOM Product Conclave and received the TechHR Spotlight Award
  • And we’ve never looked back since then

The EDGE Graph

  • At the heart of all our products lies our ever-evolving Smart AI EDGE GraphTM - an astute knowledge graph
  • It has analyzed over 10 million job descriptions and 35 million candidate profiles

What We Believe

  • We believe in preaching what we practice
  • EDGE keeps its people at the core of all business decisions, and the same is reiterated through our array of talent management products. It is in our DNA
  • We strive for a ‘culture of performance’ wherein each one is responsible and accountable for their own work and performance and mentors one another
  • We firmly believe that we are nothing without the people we work with

The EDGE edge

  • Our values have helped us grow and sustain in the era of cut-throat competition
  • We have been fortunate enough to attract the right mix of talent across the entire gamut of the business
  • Today, we are a team of svelte, deep-rooted and incredibly talented professionals who are ready to take EDGE to the next level
