    Life at EDGE
    To help you find the job you love, we start by loving our own jobs first! It’s the unmistakable feel of a workplace focused on openness to ideation, creativity and innovation. And talented people loving what they do.

    Add to that some fierce passions flying around, fearless experimentation and fervent energy that is conspicuous the moment you walk through our doors. That’s the reason we like to think of ourselves as Workforce Warriors!
    Our Culture
    The culture at EDGE encourages you to think and see things differently and find new ways to make the magic happen. It’s a lean team, which means the responsibilities are that much more individually, and yet, we support each other to achieve our goals and dreams.

    Whether it is sales and marketing, product, operations, human resources, data scientists, analysts, engineers – we strive to learn, take risks, come up with new ideas in HR technology – and take everyone along to the next level. And all of this is done with loads of camaraderie, laughter, parties (and yes, some occasional hysteria and flying tempers) to keep each of us thriving.
    On The Cutting Edge
    Our developers work with the most advanced technologies to build and write quality code while thinking about scale at every push. Our data science team is all about unearthing the possibilities of Natural Language Processing, machine learning, predictive modelling and visualizing real-time data. They love crunching numbers so much that you’ll often find them hosting math competitions! (we’re told the math goes well with beer)

    Learning, unlearning and relearning is the EDGE way of life. We strive to stay up to date with the latest trends but sometimes even lead the trends ourselves. Like the data we work with, structured and unstructured, we make magic happen in many different ways! We look at the people-related challenges enterprises face today – from many angles –to provide solutions that make our clients go wow!
