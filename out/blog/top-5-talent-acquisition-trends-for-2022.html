    Top 5 Talent Acquisition Trends For 2022

    As the year comes to an end, this week let’s take a look at the top 5 talent acquisition trends that defined 2021 and those that will pave the way for 2022.

    1. Data-driven recruiting to grow in 2022 too! Data will be king and will define the way recruitment technologies are used.

    2. AI-based recruitment tools to match the right candidate with the right job will improve and grow

    3. Personalized nurturing will continue to provide a premier candidate experience

    4. Diversity, Inclusion and equity will remain an acquisition priority

    5. Employee referrals & Internal mobility will be important aspects of acquiring the right talent.
