    Marching Together Towards the Future of Technology @NVIDIA GTC 2017

    GPU Technology Conference organized by NVIDIA (a leader in AI and visual computing) is the largest and most important event of the year for GPU developers. GTC showcases the most innovative work in the computing industry today—including artificial intelligence and deep learning, virtual reality, self-driving cars and accelerated analytics. No surprise that this much-awaited event is buzzing with activity and we couldn’t be more thrilled to be part of it!

    Via prestigious platforms like GTC, NVIDIA also aims to promote startups that are doing remarkable work in the areas of artificial intelligence, virtual reality, autonomous vehicles and robotics. We, the EdGE Networks team, have flown all the way from Bangalore to see how the cutting-edge GPU technologies are creating amazing innovations and discoveries and transforming various industries.

    EdGE Networks, was among the top 10 Indian startups in the GPU space and it won “The Best of Show” awards at the Emerging Companies Summit (ECS) hosted by NVIDIA as part of its flagship GPU Technology Conference (GTCx) India 2016. We were recognized for harnessing the potential of AI and Data Science to solve workforce challenges and won award prizes worth $200,000. As a next gen HR technology provider, our strength is in harnessing data science using RNNs on high-end GPUs combined with NLP to solve the toughest talent acquisition and workforce optimization challenges faced by organizations. And we’re here in San Jose to share our story!

    To learn more, join us at the 2017 GPU Technology Conference (GTC) from May 8-11 in San Jose, California at Booth 1018.
