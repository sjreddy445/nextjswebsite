    Life @ EdGE
    Five years and going strong...

    Yes, another milestone for us at EdGE Networks- we turned five years old on July 19, 2015.
    We have had our fair share of highs and lows at EdGE, and our employees, customers, partners and vendors have stood by us, to help our company grow. We would like to take this opportunity, to thank each and every one of you for being a part of EdGE Networks’ growth.

    Here is what some of our first few employees have to say about EdGE.

    “Believing in yourself, is what works for me, at EdGE. The most exciting moment was signing a contract with our first customer, after competing with billion dollar software giants. The lows were when people did not want to join us when they saw our first office, which was an apartment! But those who did take the leap are our champions today”, says Arun Kumar K, Head- Operations.

    Arjun Pratap, Founder & CEO says, “Words that come to my mind when talking about this journey are riveting, explosive and testing. I have come to learn about people, faith and friendship, and most importantly the meaning of change. From a fledgling to its current state, I have seen the evolution of an idea to execution, with moments of extinction in between. Starting a company in India, building and selling products and solutions to the Indian customer are an unparalleled experience. The most important facet of my learning is the people that build our team, the soul of the entity we call EdGE Networks, a myriad of hope, expression, want and fulfilment. It is this passion and soulful energy that I wake to every morning of the week and begin a beautiful, bright day. Thank you people, thank you friends and family, thank you all for having the guts to walk through the door and be a part of a new beginning.”

    Priyadarshi Lahiri, Head- Technology, says, “I have been at EdGE now for 3 years. And it is in this time that I have truly understood who I really am as a person and what my true calling is. It has been an extremely exciting journey building a platform that has the potential to define Recruitment and Workforce Optimization 2.0 from how enterprises see it today. Building trust with our customers, building a great application, the data science team innovating to create better offerings, and serving our customers better is what has kept me and my team busy and we have loved every moment of it.”

    Great going, EdGE Networks! Keep the faith and reach for the stars.
