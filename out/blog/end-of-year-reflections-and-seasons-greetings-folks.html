    End-of-year reflections and Seasons’ Greetings, Folks!

    Yes, it is that time of the year again. Another year has gone by, and so much has happened, in India, globally and especially in the adrenaline-charged world of in technology. The conversations emphasis continue to revolve around is on the Internet of Things, the convergence of cloud and mobile computing, wearable devices, and smart devices talking to each other and more. In a nutshell, technology is rapidly evolving to transform our lives in unimaginable ways, getting smarter and more useful as they do. Through it all, the evolution of digital business is clearly at the heart of what is covered.

    Technology management spends are expected to grow at a decent and steady pace. However, spend on business technology — the things that let firms win, serve, and retain customers — is expected to be double!

    A part of this technology investment is in Human Resources, and many businesses are asking relevant questions about HR technology like talent acquisition and management tools. Are the right people with the necessary skills being recruited? Are the relevant candidates being targeted? After recruiting the person, will he/ she be the right fit for the job? Are we doing the right things to stem employee attrition and enhancing the potential of our workforce? How can we sharpen our efforts with data science and analytics?

    As organizations up their game in 2016 and harness HR technology to optimize their workforce, big disruptions on the HR horizon are expected for businesses to have a successful 2016. And EdGE Networks is here to help you take your business to the next level.

    As this year comes to a close, we take this opportunity to thank you for your support, encouragement and collaboration with EdGE Networks. We look forward to working closely together and building on our successes.

    Season’s greetings and best wishes for a joyous and prosperous 2016!
